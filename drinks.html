The simple mix of white rum, lime juice, cane sugar, and soda (with muddled mint, please) is fresh and tropical, and it’s a classic that we don’t expect to disappear any time soon.

CAIPIRINHA

The cocktail is made with Brazil’s national spirit, cachaça, along with sugar and lime.

MOSCOW MULE

This famous mug-dwelling drink contains ginger, vodka, lime, and soda.
